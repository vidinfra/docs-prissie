# Optimize your Image

The Image Optimizer feature automatically optimizes your images in real-time to enhance site performance. 

It **reduces image size** without **compromising quality**, **speeds up load times**, and **saves bandwidth**, **ensuring faster page delivery** across all devices.

## 1. Turning On Image Optimizer
- To enable **Image Optimizer**, click on the **Turn on Image Optimizer** button. 

    <Frame caption="Turn on Image Optimizer">
        <img src="/images/cdn/31.png" alt="Origin" />
    </Frame>

## 2. Image Optimization in Action
- After enabling the optimizer, the status will change to **Image Optimizer is Active**.
    
    <Frame caption="Image Optimizer in Action">
        <img src="/images/cdn/33.png" alt="Origin" />
    </Frame>

## 3. Turning Off Image Optimizer
- If you want to stop the image optimization, you can simply click on the **Turn Off** button. This will deactivate the Image Optimizer, and your images will no longer be automatically compressed or transformed by the CDN.

    <Frame caption="Turn off Image Optimizer">
        <img src="/images/cdn/32.png" alt="Origin" />
    </Frame>
