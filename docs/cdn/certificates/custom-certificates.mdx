# Add your Custom SSL Certificate

**SSL Certificate** section allows you to securely upload and manage SSL certificates for your domain, ensuring all data is transmitted over HTTPS.

## Upload a New SSL Certificate

### 1. Click on New Certificate 
- To add a new SSL certificate, click the **+ New Certificate** button.

    <Frame caption="Add new SSL">
        <img src="/images/cdn/38.png" alt="Origin" />
    </Frame>

### 2. Enter Certificate Details
    - **Label**: Give your certificate a name for easy identification.
    - **Certificate**: Paste the PEM-formatted certificate that you have received from a Certificate Authority (CA), including the BEGIN CERTIFICATE and END CERTIFICATE tags.
    - **Private Key**: Paste the private key associated with your certificate, ensuring it is in PEM format.
    - **Chain**: If your certificate includes intermediate certificates, paste them in the Chain section, also in PEM format.

    <Frame caption="Enter Details">
        <img src="/images/cdn/39.png" alt="Origin" />
    </Frame>

### 3. Save the Certificate
After entering all required information, click **Save** to upload and secure your domain.

    <Frame caption="Save the Certificate">
        <img src="/images/cdn/40.png" alt="Origin" />
    </Frame>
