This guide will walk you through the simple process of creating your **Tenbyte Cloud account**, allowing you to access our powerful cloud infrastructure services. 

## Prerequisites

Before starting, make sure you have:

- A **Tenbyte account** â€” [Sign up](https://console.tenbytecloud.com/app/).
- A project or application that you want to deploy.
- Basic knowledge of cloud computing concepts.

## Sign up for a account
- Visit the [Tenbyte Website](https://beta.tenbyte.io/).
- On the homepage, click the "Sign Up with Email" button. This will take you to the cloud sign-in page.

    <Frame caption="Tenbyte Website">
        <img src="/images/cloud/1.png" alt="Tenbyte Website" />
    </Frame>

- Click **Create an Account** to create your account. A new page will open.

    <Frame caption="Create your account">
        <img src="/images/cloud/3.png" alt="Create your account" />
    </Frame>

- **Add Your Email Address**: Enter a valid email address.
- **Create a Password**: Set a secure password that meets the following requirements:
    - At least 8 characters long
    - Includes one uppercase letter
    - Includes one special character
- Once all fields are filled, click **Sign up** to create your account,
- A email will be delivered to your given email so you can verify your account.

- Once you have confirmed your email, you will be able to access your **Tenbyte Cloud dashboard**.

    <Frame caption="Tenbyte Cloud Dashboard">
        <img src="/images/cloud/2.png" alt="Tenbyte Cloud Dashboard" />
    </Frame>
