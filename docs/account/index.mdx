# Manage your account settings
Welcome to the [Tenbyte Account settings](https://beta.tenbyte.io/) page. Here you can review how the Tenbyte dashboard is organized and learn how to configure your account features.

From the left-side menu, you can view all pages that explain your **Tenbyte account** features.

1. [Account Creation](/docs/account/account-creation): Create a new Tenbyte account and set up your initial dashboard access.
2. [Profile Settings](/docs/account/profile-settings): Update your name, email, and other basic details linked to your Tenbyte account.
3. [Session Management](/docs/account/session-management): View active logins, monitor connected devices, and terminate unwanted sessions.
