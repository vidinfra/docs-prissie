The Response Classes (Non 2xx) section tracks the number of HTTP responses that fall outside the successful 2xx range, including errors like 4xx (client-side) and 5xx (server-side) errors.

    <Frame caption="Response Classes">
        <img src="/images/cdn/44.png" alt="Response Classes" />
    </Frame>

## What it tells you:

- The number of non-2xx responses, such as 4xx (client-side errors) and 5xx (server-side errors).
- How many failed requests are occurring on your CDN, indicating potential issues with content delivery.
- The distribution between client errors (4xx) and server errors (5xx).

--- 

## How to read it:
- The main numbers show the total count of 4xx and 5xx requests.
- The colored bars represent the volume of each type of error, with 4xx errors shown in yellow and 5xx errors in red.
- The graph displays how these errors fluctuate over time, helping you monitor issues and trends in CDN performance.
