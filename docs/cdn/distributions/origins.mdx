# Manage your CDN Origins
In this section, you can add, edit, or remove origin sources for your CDN distribution. 

1. **Hostname**: You can add an origin by specifying the hostname.
2. **S3 Bucket**: For cloud storage, you can use an S3 Bucket as your origin.

## Steps to Add an Origin:
### 1. Choose Your Origin Type
Select either **Hostname** or **S3 Bucket** to define the type of origin you want to use.

### 2. Configure Your Origin

- **For Hostname**: Enter the **Origin Name** and the **Domain / IP address**.

    </Frame>

- **For S3 Bucket**: Enter the **Origin Label**, **Endpoint**, **Access Key ID**, **Secret Access Key**, **Bucket Name**, and **Region** for your S3 origin.
