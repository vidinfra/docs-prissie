#  What is Tenbyte CDN?

**Tenbyte CDN** is a global content delivery network that improves website, application, and video performance by serving content from edge servers close to your users. 

It reduces latency, speeds up load times, and handles high traffic with ease. Tenbyte CDN also includes multi-CDN routing, image optimization, low-latency streaming, and real-time analytics, making it a strong fit for media platforms and high-demand digital services.

---

## Why Tenbyte CDN

- **Smart Routing:** Automatically routes users to the nearest, healthiest edge node.
- **Global Reach:** Dozens of PoPs across regions for low-latency delivery.
- **Custom Rules:** Define cache behavior, headers, and access rules at the edge.
- **Secure by Default:** TLS, WAF integration, token-based access, and signed URLs.
- **Real-Time Insights:** Track bandwidth, requests, cache hit ratio, and more.
- **Seamless Integration:** Connect directly with **Vidinfra**, **Image Optimization**, and **Tenbyte Cloud**.
- **High Traffic Capacity:** Built to handle large spikes and enterprise-scale traffic demands.
- **HTTP/2 and HTTP/3 Support:** Modern protocols for faster connection setup and efficient delivery.
- **Instant Cache Purge:** Remove outdated files from the edge in real time.
- **Flexible Billing:** Pay-as-you-go pricing with predictable usage-based costs.
- **Multi-CDN Capability:** Automatically switches between networks for better speed and uptime.
