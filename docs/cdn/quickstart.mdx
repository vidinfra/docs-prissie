## Set Up Your CDN Workspace

- To learn how to create a Tenbyte account, check out the [Account Creation Guide](/docs/account/account-creation#sign-up-for-a-tenbyte-account) in the Account and Organization section.

- After signing in with your registered email and password, you will see a welcome page.
