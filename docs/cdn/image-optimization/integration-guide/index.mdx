These guides explain how to connect Tenbyte’s image optimization service to your projects. 

We’ve included the platforms developers use most often. If the one you’re working with isn’t listed, you can contact our support team and we’ll help you with the integration.

- [**Next.js**](/docs/cdn/image-optimization/integration-guide/nextjs) — Add Tenbyte image handling to your Next.js project for automatic optimization and edge delivery during server-side and static renders.
- [**React Native**](/docs/cdn/image-optimization/integration-guide/react-native) — Use Tenbyte with React Native to fetch optimized images at runtime and reduce bandwidth for mobile apps.
- [**Android and iOS apps**](/docs/cdn/image-optimization/integration-guide/android-ios) — Integrate Tenbyte Image Optimization into native mobile apps to deliver smaller images and faster load times on mobile networks.
- [**Shopify**](/docs/cdn/image-optimization/integration-guide/shopify) — Connect Tenbyte to your Shopify store to speed up product images and improve storefront performance.
- [**Astro.js**](/docs/cdn/image-optimization/integration-guide/astro) — Plug Tenbyte into Astro to optimize images at build time and serve lightweight assets to end users.
- [**Nuxt**](/docs/cdn/image-optimization/integration-guide/nuxt) — Configure Tenbyte in Nuxt to optimize images during build and serve them efficiently from the edge.
