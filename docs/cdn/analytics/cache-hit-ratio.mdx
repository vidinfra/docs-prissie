The Cache Hit Rate shows the percentage of requests served directly from the cache versus the origin server.

    <Frame caption="Cache Hit Rate">
        <img src="/images/cdn/43.png" alt="Cache Hit Rate" />
    </Frame>

## What it tells you:

- How efficiently your content is cached.
- Whether viewers are experiencing faster response times.
- How much load is reduced on the origin server.

--- 

## How to read it:

- Higher percentages indicate better content caching.
- A higher cache hit rate means faster playback and reduced origin server usage.
