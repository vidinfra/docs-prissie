# General Settings

The General Settings page lets you manage the core information for your Tenbyte organization.

## 1. Organization Information

- This section contains the primary details of your Tenbyte workspace.

    <Frame caption="Organization Information">
        <img src="/images/account/23.png" alt="Organization Information" />
    </Frame>

    - **Name**- Update the official name of your organization or team.
    - **Organization ID**- A unique identifier automatically generated for your organization.

        <Tip>
        You can copy it with a single click. 
        </Tip>

    - **Organization Logo**- Upload a logo to help personalize your workspace.

---

## 2. Delete your Organization
Actions in this section are permanent and cannot be undone. 

    <Frame caption="Delete your Organization">
        <img src="/images/account/24.png" alt="Delete your Organization" />
    </Frame>

Deleting your organization permanently removes:

    - All videos
    - All CDN distributions
    - Live streams
    - Analytics data
    - Transcoding profiles
    - All members and permissions

<Warning>
    Select Delete Organization only if you’re sure you want to remove everything linked to this workspace.
</Warning>

---

## 3. Saving Changes

At the bottom of the page, you’ll see the update bar. Click **Update Organization** after modifying any fields to apply your updates.
