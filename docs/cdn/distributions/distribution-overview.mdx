# Distribution Overview

The Distribution Overview section allows you to view essential information about your Tenbyte CDN distribution. Here you can monitor the status, domain, and configuration of your CDN distribution. 

- To **manage your distribution**, click the **Action** button next to your specific CDN distribution and select **Manage** from the dropdown menu.

    <Frame caption="Manage your Distribution">
        <img src="/images/cdn/13.png" alt="Manage your Distribution" />
    </Frame>

- A dashboard for your created CDN distribution will appear, allowing you to manage and configure various settings for your distribution.

    <Frame caption="CDN Distribution Dashboard">
        <img src="/images/cdn/14.png" alt="CDN Distribution Dashboard" />
    </Frame>

- Select **Overview** from the left sidebar, then fill in the required fields such as the Distribution Name and Description to complete the configuration of your CDN distribution.

## Distribution Details
This section provides basic information about your CDN distribution.

    <Frame caption="CDN Distribution Details">
        <img src="/images/cdn/16.png" alt="CDN Distribution Details" />
    </Frame>

- **Domain**: The Domain field displays the unique domain name assigned to your CDN distribution.

<Tip>
    You can click Copy Domain to easily copy the domain name to your clipboard.
</Tip>

- **Status**: The Status shows the current state of the distribution. It can show various statuses such as **`Provisioning`**  or **`Deployed`**.
- **Created On**: This section shows the date and time when the distribution was created.
- **Last Modified**: The Last Modified field shows when the distribution's settings were last updated.

## Configuration
Here, you can modify the basic configuration of your distribution, including the domain name and other settings as needed for your specific use case.

    <Frame caption="CDN Distribution Configuration">
        <img src="/images/cdn/17.png" alt="CDN Distribution Configuration" />
    </Frame>

    - **Distribution Name**: You can assign a name to your CDN distribution for easier identification.
    - **Description**: Optionally, you can provide a brief description to describe the distribution, helping you identify it in the future.
