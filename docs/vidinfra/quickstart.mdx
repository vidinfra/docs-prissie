This guide helps you upload, process, and play your first video with Vidinfra, a complete cloud platform for hosting and streaming. 

In a few simple steps, you’ll turn a raw video file into a ready-to-stream playback link.

---

## ⚙️ Prerequisites

Before starting, make sure you have:

- A **Tenbyte account** — [Sign up](https://beta.tenbyte.io/vidinfra)
- A **video file** ready to upload (e.g. `.mp4`, `.mov`, `.mkv`)
- An **API key** with `assets:write` permission

---

## Set Up Your Vidinfra Workspace

- After signing in with your registered email and password, you will see a welcome page.
    
    <Frame caption="Vidinfra Dashboard">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/dashboard.png" alt="Vidinfra Dashboard" />
    </Frame>

- From the left sidebar, select **Vidinfra**.
- A workspace will open. From here, create your library by selecting **New Library**.
    
    <Frame caption="Library Workspace">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/library.png" alt="Library Workspace" />
    </Frame>

- Fill in the required fields with your information, including the **library name**, **region**, and **purpose of the library**, then click **Create Library** to continue.
    
    <Frame caption="Create Video Library">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/library-01.png" alt="Create Video Library" />
    </Frame>

    <Note>Choose your preferred region to ensure the best performance and streaming quality.</Note>

- Once the library is created successfully, your workspace will display the video dashboard. 

    <Frame caption="Video Library Dashboard">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/library-04.png" alt="Video Library Dashboard" />
    </Frame>    

From here, you can **upload** or **import** videos to start building your content library.

## Upload your Video

- Click **Upload Video** to add a new file from your device and start building your library.

    <Frame caption="Upload your Video">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/library-02.png" alt="Upload your Video" />
    </Frame>

- Alternatively, choose from the supported platforms to bring your existing videos directly into Vidinfra.

    <Frame caption="Import Existing Videos">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/library-03.png" alt="Import Existing Videos" />
    </Frame>

## Stream your Video

- Vidinfra automatically processes your video with high-speed optimization, transcoding it into multiple renditions and adaptive formats (**`HLS`** and **`DASH`**) to make it stream-ready within seconds.

    <Frame caption="Vidinfra Processing your Video">
        <img src="/images/vidinfra/library-05.png" alt="Vidinfra Processing your Video" />
    </Frame>

> ✅ Supported formats: `.mp4`, `.mov`, `.mkv`, `.webm`, `.avi`, `.flv`

- Once your video is uploaded, you can start using it across your business for streaming, embedding, or sharing with your audience.

## 1️⃣ Install the SDK

Vidinfra offers official SDKs for Node.js, Python, and other languages.
Here’s how to install the Node SDK:

```bash
npm install @vidinfra/sdk
```
