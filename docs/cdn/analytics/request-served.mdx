The Request Served section shows the total number of requests successfully handled by your CDN. 

    <Frame caption="Requests Served Staistics">
        <img src="/images/cdn/41.png" alt="Traffic Served Staistics" />
    </Frame>

## What it tells you:

- The number of requests successfully handled by your CDN.
- How frequently your content is being accessed by users.

--- 

## How to read it:

- The main number shows the total number of requests served by the CDN.
- The percentage indicates the change in request volume compared to the previous time period.
