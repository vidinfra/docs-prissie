The **Tenbyte SDK** enables developers to integrate Tenbyte's video hosting and streaming services into their applications.

The **Vidinfra SDK** provides developers with all the tools necessary to **build** and **manage high-quality video streaming solutions** while ensuring **seamless integration** with Tenbyte's powerful  infrastructure.

Available SDKs for various platforms include:

- [**HTML5 (Web)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/html5)
- [**Video.js (Web)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/video-js) 
- [**HLS.js (Web)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/hls-js) 
- [**Shaka Player (Web)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/shaka-player) 
- [**React Player (Web)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/react-player) 
- [**React Native Player (iOS + Android)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/react-native-player) 
- [**AVPlayer (iOS)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/av-player)
- [**ExoPlayer (Android)**](/docs/vidinfra/player-sdks/exo-player)
