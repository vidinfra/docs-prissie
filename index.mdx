# Start building with Tenbyte

Welcome to the **Tenbyte Developer Hub**. This page help you to understand what you can build with Tenbyte and guide you to the right tools without slowing you down. 

## What is Tenbyte?

Tenbyte is a cloud and video infrastructure provider operating with Tier III data centers and enterprise-grade hardware. It focuses on reliable compute, S3-compatible T2 block storage, global CDN delivery, and a complete video platform for streaming and media workflows

## Why Users Choose Tenbyte
Tenbyte keeps things simple:

- Pricing is predictable and billed in differrnt local currency.
- Tier III data centers and enterprise hardware provide steady performance.
- Compute, storage, CDN, and video tools live in one place.
- A regional support team is available at any hour.

> These points make Tenbyte easier to work with compared to platforms that depend on international routing or currency conversion.
