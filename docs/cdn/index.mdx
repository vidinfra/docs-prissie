**Tenbyte CDN** is a global content delivery network that speeds up websites, applications, and video delivery by serving content from edge locations closer to users. 

It reduces latency, improves load times, and handles high traffic efficiently, with multi CDN routing, image optimization, low latency streaming, and built in analytics for media heavy platforms.

## Why Tenbyte CDN

- **Smart Routing:** It automatically sends users to the nearest edge node which is also healthy.
- **World Wide Coverage**: Numerous PoPs within regions for quick delivery.
- **Custom Rules:** Set cache behavior, headers and access rules at the edge.
- **Secure by Default:** TLS, WAF integration, token based access and signed URLs.
- **Real-Time Insights:** Track bandwidth, requests, cache hit ratio and more.
- **Seamless Integration:** Get in touch with Vidinfra, Image Optimization and Tenbyte Cloud.
- **High Traffic Capacity:** Designed to manage high volumes of traffic and demand.
- **HTTP/2 and HTTP/3 Support:** Newer protocols are designed for faster connection setup and delivery.
- **Instant Cache Purge:** Remove outdated files from the edge in real time.
- **Flexible Billing:** Pay as you go pricing with predictable usage based costs.
- **Multi-CDN Function**: Automatically switches networks based on performance and uptime.
