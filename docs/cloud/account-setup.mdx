# Editing Your Profile

This page allows you to update your Tenbyte Cloud account details. You can modify your personal information, change your password, and manage your account settings.

## 1. Access Your Profile
- From the right side of the dashboard, click on your **Account name**. A dropdown menu will appear.
- From the dropdown, select **Profile**. This will take you to your **Profile Dashboard**.

    <Frame caption="Access your Tenbyte Profile">
        <img src="/images/cloud/6.png" alt="Access your Tenbyte Profile" />
    </Frame>

## 2. Edit your Tenbyte Profile
- From the **Profile Dashboard** you can configure following things 

    <Frame caption="Edit your Profile">
        <img src="/images/cloud/7.png" alt="Edit your Profile" />
    </Frame>

    - **Name**: Your first and last name.
    - **Email**: The email address linked to your Tenbyte Cloud account.
    - **Password**: Option to reset your password.
    - **Created At**: The date and time your account was created.

## 3. Users I Can Access
- This section shows the users you have access to within Tenbyte Cloud. 

## 4. Users Who Can Access My Account
- Here, you can manage users who have access to your account. 

## 5. Delegating New Access
- You can delegate access to other users by clicking the “**+ Delegate new access**” button. 
To learn more about **delegating new access**, check out the [Manage Access](/docs/cloud/networking/manage-access) page under the **Networking and Security** section.
